Welcome to my creative page!

This is a page where I will showcase one of my passions: electronic music production!

As I got more comfortable with the tools, I started to create full songs, dreaming of creating music like other producers I would listen to. During COVID, I had a lot of time to work on music production, and making a lot of songs. I am still learning all the time, and my current goal is to have a carrer composing and producing sound tracks and sound effects for video games.
